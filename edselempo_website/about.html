About Me

I’m Edwin Selempo, a professional safari guide with over 30 years of experience leading unforgettable wildlife expeditions across East Africa. I specialize in full-round guiding, with deep, hands-on knowledge of wildlife behavior, tracking, and local ecosystems.

Whether it’s watching lions hunt at dawn, identifying bird calls at sunset, or sharing stories around a campfire, I’m here to give you an authentic and safe African safari experience.

On this site, you’ll find real trip stories, photo highlights, and moments that reflect the beauty and thrill of the African wilderness.
